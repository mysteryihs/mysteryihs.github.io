An End to Game of Thrones

A final conclusion to the 8 year old series and my thoughts on it.

  • Season 8 was a flaming pile of garbage.
  • The second half of Game of Thrones had a huge decline in quality overall, people just didn't realize it until it season 8.
  • The show went downhill once it went past the books and GRRM left.
  • A quick look at the imdb page shows people rate the show a 10/10 despite all this. Really?
  • Overall I'm glad the show is finally over. After a few months of discussion we can finally move on.
